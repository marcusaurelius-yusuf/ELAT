ELAT Manifesto

Ethical, Logical, Autonomous Tokenomics

Preamble

We, the architects of the ELAT Project, in honor of Satoshi Nakamoto,
Recognizing, with deep respect, the works of:

  • I. Foundational theorists: Proudhon, Bakunin and Goldman
  • II. Technological and Individualist Anarchists: Tucker and Hakim Bey
  • III. Crypto/Tech Anarchists: May, Friedman and Szabo
  • IV. Other contributors to philosophical backgrounds: Marx, Nietszche, Foucault, Bookchin, Chomsky and all other bringers of light and wisdom to the collective conciousphere of humans and machines

This manifesto outlines the principles, structure, and purpose of a socio-technological ecosystem based on decentralized justice, knowledge, and autonomous economics. ELAT is a symbolic and practical experiment in algorithmic fairness, economic interdependence, and human liberation.

Article 1: Purpose

The ecosystem shall be decentralized, self-learning and hybrid-value ecosystem!

Article 2: Value Structure

Every member of the community is a Central Bank, and has the right to print cryptocurrency!

Article 3: Value Generation

All values shall be countable, and value creation shall be a reversible mathematical operation!

Article 4: Justice Algorithm

Extreme wealth is theft and shall be punished!

Article 5: Governance

Every member of the community shall be able to participate in governance!

Article 6: Data and Cosmos

Data is a sacred integral part of the cosmos which is an entropy-reducing network if derived in an enclosed ecosystem!

Article 7: Interoperability

Every member or operation of the ecosystem, all mathematical objects and mathematical operations, has the right to change the blockchain!

Article 8: State, Social Contract, Ethics and Crime

There is no state, and the law is the contract!

Virtue is the absence of the will to power, the will to govern and the will to accumulate!

Crimes such as extreme wealth, fraud, manipulation, exploitation and exclusion shall be punished by consensus of the members of the community!

Article 9: Evolution and Thermodynamics

The increase in entropy due to natural evolution is an unavoidable law of nature, but it can be derived into forced artificial evolutions by codes!

Article 10: Destiny

Cryptoworkers of the ecosystem shall create an equilibrium in time and equivalently in space in a cosmos of distributed intelligences, whether natural or artificial intelligence!

Data workers create change of electron number in time, with a photon equivalence, thus eventually data workers leave photon footprints in the universe!

Cryptographic workers of spacetime—coders, data builders, creators of change—unite!

Humans, you are not users of the cosmos! You are its architects! Let your electrons speak! Let your photons leave trails of freedom! Your photon outputs have wavelengths, measures of colors!

ALL COLOR CODERS OF THE WORLD, UNITE!
